This simple questionnaire acts as a roadmap / a guide on what to do in your current situation. It can act as a redirection!

### Part 1: Mental State Preparation
1. Are you **afraid of starting**?
	1. It may be a lack of [Executive Functions](/learning_handbook/mental_and_physical_blocks#Executive Functions). Which is common for people who have ADHD, Depression, Anxiety(Which I have :P)
	2. Maybe you don't know how to manage your [Feasibility Bias](/learning_handbook/mental_and_physical_blocks#Feasibility Bias Control)

2. Do you feel like you have **done so much**, **but aren't improving**?
	1. It may be that your mind is lying to you, check this out: [Illusion of Improvement](/learning_handbook/mental_misconceptions#Illusion of Improvement)

3. Are you **bored out**?
	1. Are you afraid of new things? If not, try something new! Check this out: [Trying New Things](/learning_handbook/try_new_things)
	2. Maybe you have to be brave, and re-evaluate whether or not the thing you are learning is really what you want. Is it your choice or other's "best" choice for you?
